# Nonresident Alien Status

## Overview

A nonresident alien (NRA) is an individual who is neither a U.S. citizen nor a resident alien under the green card test or substantial presence test. NRAs are taxed only on:

1. Income from sources within the United States
2. Income effectively connected with a U.S. trade or business

## Determination

You are a nonresident alien if you do **not** meet either:

- **Green Card Test** - Lawful permanent resident status
- **Substantial Presence Test** - 31/183 day formula

## Tax Treatment

Nonresident aliens face different tax treatment depending on income type:

### Effectively Connected Income (ECI)
- Taxed at graduated rates (same as U.S. citizens)
- Deductions allowed against ECI
- Must be engaged in U.S. trade or business

### Fixed, Determinable, Annual, or Periodical (FDAP) Income
- Taxed at flat 30% rate (or lower treaty rate)
- No deductions allowed
- Includes interest, dividends, rents, royalties, annuities

## Income Subject to Tax

| Income Type | Tax Treatment |
|-------------|---------------|
| U.S. wages | ECI - Graduated rates |
| U.S. business income | ECI - Graduated rates |
| U.S. real property gains | ECI - Graduated rates |
| U.S. dividends | FDAP - 30% or treaty rate |
| U.S. interest | FDAP - 30% or treaty rate (often exempt) |
| U.S. rents | FDAP - 30% or ECI election |
| Foreign source income | Generally not taxed |

## Filing Requirements

File [[Form 1040-NR]] if you:

1. Are engaged in a U.S. trade or business
2. Have U.S. income not fully withheld at source
3. Want to claim a refund or deductions/credits
4. Are a representative of a nonresident alien

**Due Date:**
- April 15 if receiving wages subject to withholding
- June 15 if no wages subject to withholding

## Standard Deduction

Nonresident aliens **cannot** claim the standard deduction.

**Exception:** Students and business apprentices from India eligible under Article 21(2) of the U.S.-India treaty may claim the standard deduction ($15,000 for 2025).

## Exemptions from Withholding

Certain income may be exempt from 30% withholding:

- Portfolio interest
- Income effectively connected with U.S. trade or business (file [[Form W-8ECI]])
- Treaty-exempt income (file [[Form W-8BEN]] or [[Form 8233]])
