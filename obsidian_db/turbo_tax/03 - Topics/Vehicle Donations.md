# Vehicle Donations

Donating a car, boat, or airplane to a qualified charitable organization can provide a tax deduction. Special rules determine the deductible amount based on what the charity does with the vehicle.

## What Qualifies

**Eligible Vehicles:**
- Cars, trucks, motorcycles
- Boats and other watercraft
- Airplanes
- Other motor vehicles

**Eligible Recipients:**
- Must be a [[Qualified Organizations|qualified organization]]
- Organization must provide required documentation

## How Deduction Is Determined

### If Charity Sells the Vehicle

**Deduction = Gross proceeds from sale**

**Requirements:**
- Charity must provide Form 1098-C or equivalent statement
- Statement must be received within 30 days of sale
- Must include sale date and gross proceeds

This applies even if sale price is less than FMV.

### If Charity Uses the Vehicle

**Deduction = Fair Market Value (FMV)**

**Requirements:**
- Charity must use vehicle in its exempt purpose
- Must use vehicle for significant period (not token use)
- Written statement from charity required including:
  - Intended use and duration
  - Confirmation vehicle will not be sold before use completes
  - Certification of intended use

### If Charity Gives/Sells to Needy Individual

**Deduction = Fair Market Value (FMV)**

**Requirements:**
- Sale or transfer at significantly below FMV
- Must be in direct furtherance of charity's exempt purpose
- Written statement required describing transaction

### If Charity Sells for $500 or Less

**Deduction = Lesser of $500 or FMV**

**Requirements:**
- Written statement from charity acknowledging donation
- Must include organization name, date, and location

## Form 1098-C Requirements

The charity must provide Form 1098-C or written statement containing:

| Information Required | When Needed |
|---------------------|-------------|
| Organization name, EIN | All donations |
| Donor name, address | All donations |
| Vehicle identification | All donations |
| Date of contribution | All donations |
| Description of vehicle | All donations |
| Certification of sale | If sold |
| Sale date and proceeds | If sold |
| Certification of use | If used |
| Intended duration of use | If used |

### Timing

- **If vehicle sold:** Statement within 30 days of sale
- **If vehicle used:** Statement within 30 days of contribution
- **If vehicle sold for $500 or less:** Statement at time of contribution

## Documentation You Must Keep

### Required Records

1. **Copy of Form 1098-C** or written statement from charity
2. **Proof of ownership** (title, registration)
3. **Photos of vehicle** (recommended)
4. **Odometer reading** at time of donation
5. **Method of determining FMV** (if claiming FMV)

### When to Attach Form 1098-C

Attach to your return if:
- Deduction exceeds $500
- Charity provides Form 1098-C

## Exceptions to Vehicle Rules

### Not a Vehicle Donation

These situations use regular noncash contribution rules:

| Situation | Rules Apply |
|-----------|-------------|
| Inventory donation | Dealer donating car held for sale |
| Bargain sale | Selling vehicle to charity below FMV |
| Foreclosure | Vehicle not owned outright |

### Fair Market Value Situations

FMV deduction applies if:
- Charity uses vehicle in exempt activities
- Charity gives vehicle to needy individual
- Charity makes material improvements to vehicle

## Determining Fair Market Value

### Sources for Vehicle FMV

- **Kelley Blue Book** (kbb.com)
- **NADA Guides** (nadaguides.com)
- **Edmunds** (edmunds.com)
- Classified ads for similar vehicles
- Dealer quotes

### Adjustments Required

Adjust FMV for:
- Mileage (above or below average)
- Condition (excellent, good, fair, poor)
- Optional equipment
- Local market conditions

### Not FMV

- Private party sale price (may differ from retail)
- Dealer retail asking price (may not reflect actual sale)

## Boats and Airplanes

### Boats

**FMV determination:**
- Marine survey recommended
- Consider engine hours, condition, equipment
- BUC Used Boat Price Guide
- NADA Marine Appraisal Guide

### Airplanes

**FMV determination:**
- Aircraft Bluebook Price Digest
- VREF Aircraft Value Reference
- Consider total time, engine hours, avionics

**Appraisal may be required** if deduction exceeds $5,000.

## Reporting on Tax Return

### Form 8283

**Section A** (deductions $5,000 or less):
- Complete for vehicle donations up to $5,000
- Include vehicle description and FMV

**Section B** (deductions over $5,000):
- Qualified appraisal required
- Charity must sign Part V

### Schedule A

- Report on line 12 (noncash contributions)
- Include with other charitable deductions

## Common Mistakes

| Mistake | Result |
|---------|--------|
| Not getting Form 1098-C | Deduction disallowed |
| Claiming FMV when charity sold vehicle | Limited to sale price |
| Not attaching Form 1098-C | May delay or deny deduction |
| Overstating vehicle condition | Penalty possible |
