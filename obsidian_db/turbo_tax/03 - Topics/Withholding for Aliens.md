# Withholding for Aliens

## Overview

Nonresident aliens are subject to special withholding rules on U.S. source income. Understanding these rules is essential for both taxpayers and withholding agents.

## Wage Withholding

### Standard Withholding
Wages paid to nonresident aliens for services performed in the U.S. are subject to graduated withholding, similar to U.S. citizens.

**Special Rules for NRAs:**
- Cannot claim "Exempt" status on Form W-4
- Must request withholding as if single with zero allowances
- Additional amounts may be required (see Notice 1392)

### Alternate Withholding Procedure
Nonresident alien students and teachers may use an alternate procedure by filing [[Form W-4]] with the employer to reduce withholding for:
- Deductible expenses
- Nontaxable scholarships
- Standard deduction (India treaty students only)

## 30% Withholding (FDAP Income)

Most U.S. source income that is not effectively connected with a U.S. trade or business is subject to 30% withholding (or lower treaty rate).

### Income Subject to 30% Withholding

| Income Type | Withholding Rate |
|-------------|------------------|
| Interest (non-portfolio) | 30% or treaty |
| Dividends | 30% or treaty |
| Rents | 30% or treaty |
| Royalties | 30% or treaty |
| Annuities | 30% or treaty |
| Social Security (85%) | 30% or treaty |
| Gambling winnings | 30% |

### Exceptions to 30% Withholding

File [[Form W-8ECI]] to claim exemption for:
- Income effectively connected with U.S. trade or business
- Income from real property with ECI election

**Portfolio Interest Exemption:** Interest on portfolio debt instruments is exempt from withholding (no Form required).

## Treaty-Based Withholding

### Forms Required

| Income Type | Form |
|-------------|------|
| Non-service income | [[Form W-8BEN]] |
| Personal services | [[Form 8233]] |
| Students/Teachers | Form 8233 + Appendix statement |

### Central Withholding Agreements (CWAs)

Nonresident alien entertainers and athletes may apply for reduced withholding through a CWA.

**Requirements:**
- File Form 13930 at least 45 days before event
- Provide supporting documentation
- Must agree to file tax return

**Website:** IRS.gov/CWA

## Withholding on Scholarships

| Scholarship Type | Withholding Rate |
|------------------|------------------|
| Degree candidate, non-service portion | 14% or treaty |
| Non-degree candidate | 30% or treaty |
| Service portion (wages) | Graduated rates |

## Real Property Withholding (FIRPTA)

### Standard Withholding
- **15%** of amount realized on disposition of U.S. real property interests
- **10%** if property acquired for personal residence and amount ≤ $1 million

### QIE Distributions
- **21%** withholding on distributions treated as gain from USRPI sale

### Partnership Interests
- **10%** withholding on gain from sale of partnership interest with ECI

## Partnership Withholding

Foreign partners in U.S. partnerships are subject to withholding on their share of ECI:

| Type | Withholding Rate |
|------|------------------|
| General rate | 37% (highest individual rate) |
| Long-term capital gain | 20% |
| Other specific rates | Per income type |

**Forms:**
- Partnership files [[Form 8805]] showing withholding
- Partner claims credit on [[Form 1040-NR]]

## Withholding on Pensions

Pension distributions to nonresident aliens are generally subject to 30% withholding on the taxable portion.

**Exception:** May request graduated withholding on portion attributable to U.S. services after 1986. File [[Form W-8BEN]] or [[Form 8233]].

## Estimated Tax Payments

Nonresident aliens may need to make estimated tax payments if:
- Income not subject to adequate withholding
- Expected tax liability ≥ $1,000

### Due Dates (Calendar Year)

| Installment | Due Date |
|-------------|----------|
| 1st | April 15 |
| 2nd | June 15 |
| 3rd | September 15 |
| 4th | January 15 (following year) |

**Form:** [[Form 1040-ES(NR)]]

## Refund of Withholding

To claim refund of taxes withheld in error:
- File [[Form 1040-NR]] by deadline
- Attach supporting documentation
- Include proof of residency status

### Social Security Refund (Green Card Holders)
Green card holders with 30% withholding on social security benefits can file [[Form 1040]] with:
- Copy of Form SSA-1042S
- Copy of green card
- Signed declaration of resident status
