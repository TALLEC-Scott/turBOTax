# Form 1023

## Overview

Form 1023 is the Application for Recognition of Exemption Under Section 501(c)(3) of the Internal Revenue Code. Organizations seeking recognition as tax-exempt charitable organizations must file this form (or Form 1023-EZ) with the IRS.

## Who Must File

### Required Filers
- Organizations seeking 501(c)(3) status
- Organizations formed after October 9, 1969
- Organizations wanting charitable contribution deductibility for donors

### Not Required to File
- Churches and interchurch organizations
- Integrated auxiliaries of churches
- Conventions or associations of churches
- Organizations (other than private foundations) with gross receipts ≤ $5,000 annually

## Filing Deadline

### 27-Month Rule
File within 27 months from the end of the month in which the organization was formed for retroactive recognition:
- Exemption effective from date of formation
- Donor contributions deductible from formation date

### Late Filing
- Exemption effective only from date of application
- May request discretionary extension for reasonable cause
- See Rev. Proc. 2018-15 for late application relief

## Form 1023 vs. Form 1023-EZ

| Feature | Form 1023 | Form 1023-EZ |
|---------|-----------|--------------|
| Eligibility | All 501(c)(3) organizations | Organizations with ≤ $50,000 projected annual gross receipts and ≤ $250,000 total assets |
| Filing Method | Electronic via Pay.gov | Electronic via Pay.gov |
| Fee | $600 | $275 |
| Processing Time | Several months | 2-4 weeks |
| Complexity | Full narrative responses | Streamlined eligibility worksheet |

## Information Required

### Organizational Documents
- Articles of incorporation or organization
- Bylaws or other governing documents
- Dissolution provisions

### Narrative Descriptions
1. Exempt purpose activities
2. Past, present, and planned activities
3. Sources of financial support
4. Fundraising methods
5. Relationship with other organizations

### Financial Information
- Statement of revenues and expenses (current and 3 prior years or 2 projected years)
- Balance sheet

### Compensation Information
- Officer, director, and key employee compensation
- Compensation policies and procedures

## User Fees

| Application | Fee |
|-------------|-----|
| Form 1023 | $600 |
| Form 1023-EZ | $275 |

Fees subject to change; check IRS.gov for current amounts.

## Processing Timeline

### Standard Processing
- Generally 3-6 months
- May take longer for complex applications
- IRS may request additional information

### Determination Letter
- Issued upon approval
- Effective date based on filing date
- May request specific ruling on foundation status

## After Approval

### Responsibilities
- File annual Form 990 series returns
- Maintain exempt purpose operations
- Avoid prohibited activities
- Keep records of determination letter

### Changes to Report
- Changes in activities
- Changes in governing documents
- Changes in address

## Appeals

### If Application is Denied
1. IRS issues proposed adverse determination
2. Organization may appeal within 30 days
3. Independent Office of Appeals review
4. May pursue judicial remedies

See [[Pub 557 - Tax-Exempt Status for Your Organization]] for appeal procedures.
