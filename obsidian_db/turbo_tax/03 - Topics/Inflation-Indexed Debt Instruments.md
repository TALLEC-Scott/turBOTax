# Inflation-Indexed Debt Instruments

## Overview

Inflation-indexed debt instruments (including TIPS - Treasury Inflation-Protected Securities) are debt instruments issued after January 5, 1997, whose principal is adjusted for inflation. Special OID rules apply to these instruments.

## Key Characteristics

- Principal amount adjusted based on inflation index (typically CPI)
- Interest payments vary as they're based on adjusted principal
- Both OID and interest income affected by inflation adjustments

## Types

### TIPS (Treasury Inflation-Protected Securities)
- U.S. Treasury securities
- Principal adjusted for inflation
- Semi-annual interest payments based on adjusted principal

### Other Inflation-Indexed Instruments
- Corporate inflation-indexed bonds
- Government-sponsored enterprise issues

## Tax Treatment

### Inflation Adjustments to Principal
- Increases in principal due to inflation are treated as OID
- Must be included in income as they accrue
- Reported on [[Form 1099-OID]]

### Interest Income
- Based on the inflation-adjusted principal
- May be reported on [[Form 1099-INT]] Box 3 instead of Form 1099-OID Box 2

### Deflation Adjustments
If principal decreases due to deflation:
- OID income is reduced
- May result in negative OID for the period

## OID Tables Reporting

Section I-C of the OID Tables contains:
- Inflation-indexed debt instruments
- Listed separately from regular corporate issues

## Premium and Discount Considerations

### Premium
For inflation-indexed instruments purchased at premium:
- Special rules apply under Regulations Section 1.1275-7
- Cannot simply apply regular premium rules

### Acquisition Premium
- Calculated differently due to inflation adjustments
- Consult Regulations Section 1.1275-7

### Market Discount
- Basis adjustments may be affected by inflation accruals
- Special computations required

## Form 1099-OID Reporting

| Box | Content |
|-----|---------|
| Box 1 | OID on corporate inflation-indexed instruments |
| Box 2 | Qualified stated interest (or may be on Form 1099-INT Box 3) |
| Box 8 | OID on Treasury inflation-indexed instruments |
