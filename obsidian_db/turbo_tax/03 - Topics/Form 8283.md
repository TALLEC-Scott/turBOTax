# Form 8283

Form 8283, Noncash Charitable Contributions, is used to report noncash charitable contributions when the total deduction exceeds $500. It documents donated property and supports the claimed deduction.

## When Required

### Threshold

Form 8283 is required when:
- Total noncash contribution deduction exceeds **$500**

**Important:** Combine all similar items of property donated during the year to determine if threshold is met.

### Not Required

Form 8283 is not required for:
- Cash contributions
- Noncash contributions totaling $500 or less

## Form Sections

### Section A - Deductions of $5,000 or Less

**Use Section A for:**
- Noncash contributions of $5,000 or less per item
- Publicly traded securities (regardless of value)
- Items in a group of similar items totaling $5,000 or less

**Information Required:**
1. Name and address of qualified organization
2. Date of contribution
3. Date organization acquired property (if sold within 3 years)
4. Description of property
5. Fair market value
6. Method used to determine FMV
7. Cost or other basis
8. Amount deducted

### Section B - Deductions Over $5,000

**Use Section B for:**
- Single items valued over $5,000
- Groups of similar items totaling over $5,000

**Additional Requirements:**
- Qualified appraisal required
- Appraiser must sign declaration
- Organization must sign Part V

## Qualified Appraisal Requirements

### When Required

For deductions over $5,000, except:
- Publicly traded securities
- Qualified vehicles with written acknowledgment from charity
- Certain inventory
- Intellectual property

### Appraisal Contents

Must include:
- Description of property
- Physical condition
- Date of contribution
- Terms of any agreement regarding use
- FMV on date of contribution
- Method of valuation
- Specific basis for valuation
- Appraiser's qualifications

### Appraiser Declaration

Appraiser must sign statement that:
- Appraisal is for income tax purposes
- Appraiser understands penalty provisions
- Appraiser has not been barred from practice in past 3 years
- Appraisal meets USPAP standards

### Appraiser Qualifications

A qualified appraiser:
- Has earned appraisal designation from recognized organization, OR
- Has minimum education and 2+ years experience
- Regularly prepares appraisals for pay
- Is not an excluded individual

**Excluded Individuals:**
- The donor
- Donee organization employee
- Party to transaction (buyer, seller)
- Anyone employed by any of above

## Part V - Donee Acknowledgment

For Section B deductions, the organization must complete and sign Part V:

1. **Organization name and address**
2. **Tax identification number**
3. **Date property received**
4. **Property description**
5. **Signature and date**
6. **Official's name and title**

**Note:** If organization sells the property within 3 years, they must file Form 8282 and notify donor.

## Filing Requirements

### Attach to Return

Attach Form 8283 to:
- Form 1040 (or 1040-SR)
- Form 1040-NR
- Form 1120 (corporations)
- Form 1065 (partnerships)
- Form 1041 (estates and trusts)
- Form 990-PF (private foundations)

### Due Date

File with your tax return by the due date (including extensions).

### Penalties

**Deduction disallowed:**
- If Form 8283 not filed and required
- If qualified appraisal not obtained when required
- If appraisal attached late (unless reasonable cause)

**Accuracy-related penalty:**
- 20% penalty if value claimed 150%+ of correct value
- 40% penalty if value claimed 200%+ of correct value
- Minimum underpayment: $5,000

## Common Mistakes

| Mistake | Consequence |
|---------|-------------|
| Not filing Form 8283 when required | Deduction disallowed |
| Using Section A for $5,000+ items | Form returned; delay |
| Missing qualified appraisal | Deduction disallowed |
| Appraiser not qualified | Appraisal invalid |
| Missing organization signature | Form incomplete |
| Not describing property adequately | May be questioned |

## Special Situations

### Vehicle Donations

Attach Form 1098-C or written statement from charity:
- If deduction exceeds $500
- Copy to return, not Form 8283 (unless no Form 1098-C)

### Conservation Contributions

Additional requirements:
- FMV before and after contribution
- Conservation purpose description
- Maps, photographs, or other documentation

### Historic Structure Easements

Additional requirements:
- National Park Service project number (if applicable)
- Photographs of entire exterior
- Description of restrictions
- $500 filing fee (if over $10,000)
- Form 8283-V payment voucher

### Clothing/Household Items Not in Good Condition

For deductions over $500 on items not in good used condition:
- Qualified appraisal required
- Attach appraisal to return
