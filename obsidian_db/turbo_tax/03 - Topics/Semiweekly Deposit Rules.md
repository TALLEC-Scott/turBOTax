# Semiweekly Deposit Rules

## Overview

Employers who report more than $50,000 in employment taxes during the lookback period must use the semiweekly deposit schedule. This requires deposits within 3-5 business days after each payday based on which day of the week payroll is paid.

## Who Must Use Semiweekly Deposits

### Threshold
- Report **more than $50,000** in employment taxes during the lookback period
- Once you become a semiweekly depositor, you remain one for the rest of that year and the following year

### Lookback Period

| Tax Year | Lookback Period |
|----------|-----------------|
| 2026 | July 1, 2024 - June 30, 2025 |
| 2027 | July 1, 2025 - June 30, 2026 |

### Taxes Counted in Lookback
- Social Security taxes (employee + employer shares)
- Medicare taxes (employee + employer shares)
- Federal income tax withheld

## Deposit Schedule

### Wednesday-Thursday-Friday Payday
If payday falls on Wednesday, Thursday, or Friday:
- **Deposit due by the following Wednesday**

### Saturday-Sunday-Monday-Tuesday Payday
If payday falls on Saturday, Sunday, Monday, or Tuesday:
- **Deposit due by the following Friday**

## $100,000 Next-Day Deposit Rule

If you accumulate **$100,000 or more** in taxes on any day:
- Deposit by the **next business day**
- You immediately become a semiweekly depositor for the rest of the year and the following year

### How Accumulation Works
- Add up all taxes (income tax withheld + employee FICA + employer FICA)
- Reset to zero after each deposit period
- Each deposit period stands alone

### Example Scenario
- Monday payday: Accumulate $110,000 in taxes
- **Deposit due Tuesday** (next business day)
- Now classified as semiweekly depositor
- Tuesday payday: $30,000 accumulated
- **Deposit due Friday** (semiweekly schedule for Tue payday)

## Business Day Rules

### Legal Holidays
If due date falls on Saturday, Sunday, or legal holiday:
- Deadline extends to next business day

## EFTPS Requirements

All federal tax deposits must be made electronically via:
- **EFTPS** (Electronic Federal Tax Payment System) - Free
- **IRS Direct Pay** - Free
- **Business Tax Account** - IRS.gov/BusinessAccount
- **Same-day wire** - Through financial institution

### EFTPS Timing
- Payments accepted same-day if submitted before 3:00 PM Eastern
- Payments over $1 million must be submitted by 8:00 PM Eastern the day before

## Failure to Deposit Penalty

Penalties apply if deposits are:
- Late
- Insufficient
- Not made electronically

### Penalty Rates
- 1-5 days late: 2%
- 6-15 days late: 5%
- More than 15 days late: 10%
- Amounts still unpaid after notice: 15%

## Monthly vs. Semiweekly Comparison

| Aspect | Monthly Depositor | Semiweekly Depositor |
|--------|-------------------|---------------------|
| Lookback taxes | $50,000 or less | More than $50,000 |
| Deposit timing | 15th of following month | Within 3-5 days of payday |
| Simpler? | Yes | No |
| More frequent deposits | No | Yes |
