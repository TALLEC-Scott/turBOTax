# Excise Tax Calendar

## Overview

The Excise Tax Calendar provides specific due dates for filing returns and making deposits of federal excise taxes. It covers various excise taxes reported on different forms with varying frequencies (quarterly, monthly, annually).

## Primary Excise Tax Forms

### Form 720 - Quarterly Federal Excise Tax Return

**Purpose**: Reports a wide variety of excise taxes including:
- Environmental taxes
- Communications and air transportation taxes
- Fuel taxes
- Retail tax
- Ship passenger tax
- Manufacturers taxes

**Filing Frequency**: Quarterly

| Quarter | Months Covered | Due Date |
|---------|----------------|----------|
| Q1 | January - March | April 30 |
| Q2 | April - June | July 31 |
| Q3 | July - September | November 2 |
| Q4 | October - December | February 2 |

### Form 2290 - Heavy Highway Vehicle Use Tax Return

**Purpose**: Reports and pays federal use tax on heavy highway vehicles (55,000 lbs or more)

**Tax Period**: July 1 - June 30

**Filing Deadlines**:
| Situation | Due Date |
|-----------|----------|
| Vehicles used in July | August 31 (full year tax) |
| Vehicles first used after July | Last day of month following first use |

### Form 730 - Monthly Tax Return for Wagers

**Purpose**: Reports and pays excise tax on wagers accepted

**Filing Frequency**: Monthly
- Due by the last day of the following month

### Form 11-C - Occupational Tax and Registration Return for Wagering

**Purpose**: Register wagering activity and pay annual occupational tax

**Filing Deadline**: July 1 (annual)

## Deposit Methods

### Regular Method Taxes
Taxes reported on Form 720 that require deposits using semiweekly schedule:
- Deposit dates vary based on when tax liability is incurred
- Different rules than employment tax deposits

### Alternative Method (Communications and Air Transportation)
Used specifically for:
- Communications taxes
- Air transportation taxes

**Deposit Schedule**:
- First half of month (1st-15th): Deposit by specific date
- Second half of month (16th-end): Deposit by specific date

## Special September Deposit Rule

For regular method taxes and alternative method taxes, special rules apply in September:

### Regular Method Taxes
- If due date falls on Saturday: Deposit due preceding Friday
- If due date falls on Sunday: Deposit due following Monday

### Alternative Method Taxes
- Different period split for September deposits
- See Form 720 instructions for specific dates

## Patient-Centered Outcomes Research (PCOR) Fee

- Filed annually on Q2 Form 720 (due July 31)
- Applies to self-insured health plans and specified health insurance policies
- If only filing for PCOR fee, don't file Form 720 for other quarters

## Heavy Highway Vehicle Use Tax Details

### Tax Threshold
- Applies to vehicles 55,000 lbs or more
- Maximum tax is $550 per vehicle for full year

### Payment Options
- Full year payment for vehicles in use during July
- Prorated payment for vehicles first used after July

### Proof of Payment
- Required for vehicle registration in many states
- Stamped Schedule 1 serves as proof

## Electronic Filing Requirements

### Form 2290
- Must be filed electronically if filing 25 or more returns
- Can file electronically for fewer returns (encouraged)
- Receive stamped Schedule 1 immediately

### Form 720
- Can be filed electronically
- Paper filing also accepted
