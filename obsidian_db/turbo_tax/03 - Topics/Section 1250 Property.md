# Section 1250 Property

## Overview

Section 1250 property includes depreciable real property that is not Section 1245 property. Recapture is limited to "additional depreciation" - amounts exceeding straight-line depreciation.

## What Is Section 1250 Property

- All real property subject to depreciation
- Buildings and structural components
- Leaseholds of land or Section 1250 property
- **Excludes**: Land (not depreciable), Section 1245 property

## Additional Depreciation

### Definition
Additional depreciation = Actual depreciation - Straight-line depreciation

### For Property Held > 1 Year
Only depreciation in excess of straight-line is recaptured.

### For Property Held ≤ 1 Year
**All depreciation is additional depreciation.**

## No Additional Depreciation Situations

Additional depreciation is zero if:

| Situation | Requirement |
|-----------|-------------|
| Straight-line used | Property held > 1 year, no bonus depreciation claimed |
| Residential low-income housing | Held ≥ 16⅔ years |
| Alternate ACRS method | 15-, 18-, or 19-year real property |
| MACRS real property | Placed in service after 1986, residential or nonresidential |

## Important Note

Most modern real property uses straight-line MACRS, so:
- **No additional depreciation**
- **No Section 1250 recapture**
- However, see "Unrecaptured Section 1250 Gain" below

## Unrecaptured Section 1250 Gain

For real property sold at gain:
- Up to 25% maximum rate on accumulated depreciation
- Even if straight-line depreciation used
- Applies to "depreciation recapture" portion of gain

### Calculation
1. Total accumulated depreciation (straight-line included)
2. Limited to total gain on sale
3. Taxed at maximum 25% rate

## Recapture Rules

### Amount Recaptured as Ordinary Income
**Lesser of:**
1. Additional depreciation, OR
2. Gain realized

### Result
- Additional depreciation recaptured as **ordinary income**
- Remaining gain = [[Section 1231 Gains and Losses|Section 1231 gain]]
- Unrecaptured portion taxed at maximum 25%

## Example: Property with Accelerated Depreciation

Office building with accelerated depreciation:
- Total depreciation claimed: $100,000
- Straight-line would have been: $80,000
- Additional depreciation: $20,000

If sold at gain:
- Ordinary income: Up to $20,000
- Remainder: Section 1231 gain

## Change in Use

If Section 1250 property becomes Section 1245 property:
- Can never again be treated as Section 1250 property
- Future dispositions use Section 1245 rules
