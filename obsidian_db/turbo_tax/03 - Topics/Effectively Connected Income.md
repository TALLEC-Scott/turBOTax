# Effectively Connected Income

## Overview

Effectively Connected Income (ECI) is income that is connected with a U.S. trade or business and is taxed at graduated rates (the same rates that apply to U.S. citizens and residents).

## Trade or Business in the United States

You must be engaged in a U.S. trade or business to have ECI. Activities that constitute a trade or business include:

| Activity | Trade or Business? |
|----------|-------------------|
| Performing personal services in U.S. | **Yes** |
| Operating a business in U.S. | **Yes** |
| Partnership engaged in U.S. business | **Yes** (for partners) |
| Trading stocks through U.S. broker | **No** (safe harbor) |
| Trading for own account | **No** (unless dealer) |
| Portfolio interest/dividends | **No** |

## Types of ECI

### U.S. Source ECI

All U.S. source income (other than certain investment income) is ECI if you are engaged in a U.S. trade or business.

### Investment Income Tests

U.S. source investment income is ECI only if it passes one of these tests:

#### Asset-Use Test
Income is from assets used in, or held for use in, the U.S. trade or business.

**Examples:**
- Working capital held for business needs
- Accounts receivable from the business
- Inventory held for sale

**Note:** Stock of a corporation is generally not treated as an asset used in a trade or business.

#### Business-Activities Test
The conduct of the U.S. trade or business was a material factor in producing the income.

**Examples:**
- Dividends received by a securities dealer
- Royalties from licensing business
- Service fees from servicing business

### Foreign Source ECI

Foreign source income can be ECI if it is:

1. Attributable to a U.S. office or fixed place of business
2. The U.S. office is a material factor in producing the income
3. The income is produced in the ordinary course of business

**Types of foreign source ECI:**
- Rents/royalties for use of intangible property outside U.S.
- Dividends/interest from banking/financing business
- Gains from sale of inventory through U.S. office

## Personal Services Income

Personal services performed in the U.S. generally create ECI.

**Includes:**
- Wages, salaries, commissions
- Fees, per diem allowances
- Bonuses
- Self-employment income

**Pensions:** Distributions attributable to services performed in U.S. after 1986 are ECI, regardless of current residency status.

## U.S. Real Property Interests

Gains from disposition of U.S. real property interests are automatically treated as ECI, even if not otherwise engaged in a U.S. trade or business.

**U.S. Real Property Interest includes:**
- Real property located in U.S. or U.S. Virgin Islands
- Stock in U.S. real property holding corporation (USRPHC)

**Publicly Traded Exception:** Stock of a U.S. corporation regularly traded on an established securities market is not a USRPI unless you own more than 5% (10% for REITs).

## Transportation Income

Transportation income is ECI if:
1. You have a fixed place of business in the U.S. involved in earning the income, AND
2. At least 90% of U.S. source transportation income is from regularly scheduled transportation

Otherwise, taxed at 4% rate (not ECI).

## Partnership Interests

Gains from sale of partnership interests in partnerships engaged in U.S. trade or business are treated as ECI to the extent of the partner's share of effectively connected gain (Section 864(c)(8)).

**Withholding:** 10% of amount realized is withheld on sales of partnership interests.

## Tax Rates

ECI is taxed at the same graduated rates as U.S. citizens:

| Filing Status | 2025 Tax Brackets |
|---------------|-------------------|
| Single | 10% - 37% |
| Married Filing Separately | 10% - 37% |
| Married Filing Jointly | 10% - 37% |

**Note:** Nonresident aliens cannot use the Married Filing Jointly status unless making the election to treat nonresident spouse as resident.

## Deductions

Deductions are allowed against ECI:
- Business expenses
- IRA contributions (if ECI compensation)
- Standard deduction (generally not available to NRAs)
- Itemized deductions related to ECI
