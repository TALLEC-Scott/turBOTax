# Forms 1042 and 1042-S Reporting

## Overview

Withholding agents must report all payments subject to Chapter 3 and Chapter 4 withholding on **Form 1042-S** and file an annual return on **Form 1042**. These forms report U.S. source income paid to foreign persons and taxes withheld.

## Form 1042 - Annual Withholding Tax Return

### Purpose
Annual return reporting:
- Total tax liability under Chapters 3 and 4
- Total withholdable payments
- Deposits made during the year
- Balance due or overpayment

### Filing Requirements
- **Due date:** March 15 following the calendar year
- **E-filing required:** For returns required after January 1, 2024 (threshold: 10 or more forms)
- **Paper filing:** Only if e-filing threshold not met

### Information Required
- Employer Identification Number (EIN)
- Total payments subject to withholding
- Tax liability by type (Chapter 3, Chapter 4)
- Deposits and payments made
- Reconciliation of tax liability

## Form 1042-S - Information Return

### Purpose
Reports each payment of U.S. source income to foreign persons:
- Amount of income paid
- Tax withheld (Chapter 3 and/or Chapter 4)
- Applicable withholding rates and codes

### Copies Required
| Copy | Recipient |
|------|-----------|
| A | IRS (with Form 1042-T) |
| B | Recipient |
| C | Withholding agent |
| D | Filed with Form 1042 |

### Filing Requirements
- **Due date:** March 15 following the calendar year
- **E-filing required:** For 10 or more forms (after January 1, 2024)
- **Extended deadline:** For NQIs using alternative procedure (January 31 of filing year)

### Form 1042-T
- Transmittal for paper-filed Forms 1042-S
- Required only for paper filing
- Summarizes total income and tax withheld

## Reporting by Withholding Rate Pool

### When Permitted
- QIs assuming primary withholding responsibility
- WPs and WTs acting in that capacity

### Pool Requirements
- Same income type
- Same withholding rate (Chapter 3)
- Same chapter 4 status (if applicable)

## Pooled Reporting for QIs

QIs may report on a pooled basis for:
- Foreign account holders not requiring payee-specific reporting
- Nonqualified intermediaries (in some cases)
- Passive NFFEs with no substantial U.S. owners

## Pro-rata Basis Reporting

Required when NQI fails to provide allocation information:
- File separate Form 1042-S for each account holder
- Allocate payment pro-rata among holders
- Check "Pro-rata Basis Reporting" box

## Reporting by Intermediaries

### QI Reporting
- Pooled basis or payee-specific
- Must designate accounts for QI treatment
- Collective refund procedures available

### NQI Reporting
- Must provide payee-specific allocation
- Alternative procedure may delay allocation information
- Pro-rata reporting if fails to provide allocation

## Corrections and Amendments

### Form 1042 Amendments
- File amended Form 1042 with corrected information
- Attach explanation of changes

### Form 1042-S Corrections
- File corrected Form 1042-S with box checked for "Corrected"
- Provide corrected Copy B to recipient

## E-Filing Requirements

### Systems
- **FIRE System:** For Forms 1042-S (filing year 2024+)
- **IRIS System:** Alternative e-filing option

### File Formats
- XML format for electronic filing
- PDF filing available for small volumes
