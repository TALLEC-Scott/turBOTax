# FDAP Income

## Overview

**FDAP Income** (Fixed or Determinable Annual or Periodical income) is the primary category of income subject to Chapter 3 withholding on payments to foreign persons. Most U.S. source FDAP income paid to foreign persons is subject to 30% withholding unless reduced by treaty.

## Definition

FDAP income includes all income **except**:
- Gains from the sale of property (excluding original issue discount)
- Items excluded from gross income regardless of owner's status (e.g., tax-exempt municipal bond interest, qualified scholarships)

## Examples of FDAP Income

### Common Types
- Compensation for personal services
- **Dividends** and dividend equivalent payments
- **Interest** (including original issue discount)
- **Rents** from real or personal property
- **Royalties** from patents, copyrights, and natural resources
- **Pensions and annuities**
- **Scholarships and fellowship grants** (taxable portion)
- **Prizes, awards, and gambling winnings**

### Other FDAP Income
- REMIC excess inclusion income
- Alimony (for agreements executed before 2019)
- Publication rights
- Sales commissions
- Distributable net income of estates and trusts
- Taxes, mortgage interest, or insurance premiums paid for a nonresident alien landlord

## Not Subject to Chapter 3 Withholding

Certain FDAP amounts are exempt from Chapter 3 withholding:
- **Portfolio interest** - Interest on certain obligations in registered form
- **Bank deposit interest** - Interest on deposits with U.S. banks
- **Short-term OID** - Original issue discount on short-term obligations
- **Contingent payment bond interest** - Certain interest on contingent payment debt instruments
- **Scholarships** - Amounts for study at foreign institutions

## Not Subject to Chapter 4 Withholding

Additional exemptions for FATCA purposes:
- Interest from short-term obligations
- Payments under grandfathered obligations (outstanding as of July 1, 2014)
- Certain non-financial payments

## Periodic vs. Lump-Sum

FDAP income can be paid:
- In periodic installments (e.g., monthly royalties)
- As a single lump sum

The character as FDAP income is the same regardless of payment frequency.

## Withholding Rate

| Type | Default Rate | Treaty Rate Available |
|------|-------------|----------------------|
| Chapter 3 | 30% | Yes, varies by treaty |
| Chapter 4 | 30% | May not apply |
