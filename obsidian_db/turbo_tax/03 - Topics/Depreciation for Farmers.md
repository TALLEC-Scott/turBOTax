# Depreciation for Farmers

## Overview

Depreciation allows farmers to recover the cost of business assets over their useful life. Most farm property is depreciated under MACRS (Modified Accelerated Cost Recovery System), which includes both GDS (General Depreciation System) and ADS (Alternative Depreciation System).

## Depreciation Methods

### MACRS (General Depreciation System)
- **200% declining balance**: 3-, 5-, 7-, and 10-year farm property placed in service after 2017
- **150% declining balance**: 15- and 20-year property
- **Straight-line**: Available as election for all property

### ADS (Alternative Depreciation System)
- Required for certain property (tax-exempt use, outside U.S.)
- Uses straight-line method over longer recovery periods
- Required if electing out of uniform capitalization rules for plants with >2 year preproductive period

## Farm Property Recovery Periods

| Asset | GDS Period | ADS Period |
|-------|------------|------------|
| Farm machinery and equipment (new) | 5 years | 10 years |
| Farm machinery and equipment (used) | 7 years | 10 years |
| Cattle (dairy or breeding) | 5 years | 7 years |
| Horses (breeding, 12 years or less) | 7 years | 10 years |
| Farm buildings | 20 years | 25 years |
| Fences | 7 years | 10 years |
| Grain bins | 7 years | 10 years |
| Trees/vines bearing fruits/nuts | 10 years | 20 years |
| Water wells | 15 years | 20 years |

## Special Depreciation Allowance (Bonus Depreciation)

- **100% bonus depreciation** restored for qualified property placed in service after January 19, 2025
- Applies to tangible property with class life of 20 years or less
- Also applies to specified plants (fruit trees, etc.) planted or grafted after January 19, 2025
- Election available to opt out

## Section 179 Deduction

- Maximum deduction for 2025: **$2,500,000**
- Phase-out begins when property placed in service exceeds $4,000,000
- SUV limit: $31,300 for 2025
- See [[Section 179 Deduction]] for details

## Conventions

- **Half-year convention**: Default for most property
- **Mid-quarter convention**: If more than 40% of property placed in service in last quarter
- **Mid-month convention**: For real property
