# Publication 590-A - Contributions to Individual Retirement Arrangements (IRAs)

## Summary

IRS Publication 590-A covers the rules for contributing to Individual Retirement Arrangements (IRAs), including Traditional IRAs, Roth IRAs, and the Retirement Savings Contributions Credit (Saver's Credit). For distributions from IRAs, see [[Pub 590-B - Distributions from IRAs]].

## What's New for 2025

### IRA Contribution Limits
- **Standard limit:** $7,000 (unchanged from 2024)
- **Catch-up contribution (age 50+):** $8,000 total

### 2026 Preview
- Contribution limit increases to $7,500 ($8,600 if age 50+)
- Modified AGI limits increase for both Traditional and Roth IRAs

### Trump Accounts (New)
New type of IRA for children born 2025-2028:
- $1,000 pilot program contribution available
- Use Form 4547 to establish
- See [[Trump Accounts]] for details

## Chapters

### Chapter 1: Traditional IRAs
Covers Traditional IRA rules including eligibility, contribution limits, deductibility, rollovers, transfers, and penalties. See [[Traditional IRA]].

**Key Topics:**
- Who can open a Traditional IRA
- How much can be contributed
- Deduction limits and phaseouts
- Nondeductible contributions (Form 8606)
- Rollovers from employer plans
- Conversions to Roth IRA

### Chapter 2: Roth IRAs
Covers Roth IRA rules including eligibility, contribution limits, income phaseouts, conversions, and rollovers. See [[Roth IRA]].

**Key Topics:**
- Income limits for contributions
- Contribution limit reductions
- Conversions from Traditional IRAs
- Rollovers from employer plans

### Chapter 3: Retirement Savings Contributions Credit (Saver's Credit)
Tax credit of up to $1,000 ($2,000 if married filing jointly) for eligible contributions to retirement plans. See [[Retirement Savings Contributions Credit]].

**2025 Income Limits:**
| Filing Status | Maximum AGI |
|---------------|-------------|
| MFJ | $79,000 |
| Head of Household | $59,250 |
| Single/MFS/QSS | $39,500 |

## Key Contribution Limits (2025)

### Traditional and Roth IRA Combined Limit
| Age | Maximum Contribution |
|-----|---------------------|
| Under 50 | $7,000 |
| 50 or older | $8,000 |

### Traditional IRA Deduction Phaseouts (Covered by Employer Plan)

| Filing Status | Phaseout Range |
|---------------|----------------|
| Single/HOH | $79,000 - $89,000 |
| MFJ/QSS | $126,000 - $146,000 |
| MFS | $0 - $10,000 |

### Roth IRA Contribution Phaseouts

| Filing Status | Phaseout Range |
|---------------|----------------|
| MFJ/QSS | $236,000 - $246,000 |
| Single/HOH | $150,000 - $165,000 |
| MFS (lived with spouse) | $0 - $10,000 |
