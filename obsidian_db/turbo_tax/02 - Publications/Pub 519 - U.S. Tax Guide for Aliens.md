# Publication 519 - U.S. Tax Guide for Aliens

## Summary

IRS Publication 519 is the comprehensive tax guide for aliens (non-U.S. citizens) filing U.S. tax returns. It covers the determination of residency status, taxation rules for resident and nonresident aliens, special provisions for dual-status years, and tax treaty benefits.

**Tax Year:** 2025

## Key Concepts

This publication addresses the fundamental distinction between:
- **Resident Aliens** - Generally taxed on worldwide income like U.S. citizens
- **Nonresident Aliens** - Taxed only on U.S. source income and income effectively connected with a U.S. trade or business

## What's New for 2025

- **Qualified disability trusts:** Exemption amount is $5,100 for 2025
- **New deductions:** No tax on tips, overtime, car loan interest, enhanced senior deduction (generally not available to NRAs)
- **Section 2801 tax:** New guidance on tax on gifts/bequests from covered expatriates
- **Russia treaty suspension:** Partial suspension effective August 16, 2024
- **Belarus treaty suspension:** Partial suspension effective December 17, 2024
- **Hungary treaty termination:** Effective January 1, 2024 for withholding
