# Publication 509 - Tax Calendars

## Summary

IRS Publication 509 provides comprehensive tax calendars showing due dates for filing tax forms, paying taxes, and taking other actions required by federal tax law. It contains three main calendars covering different taxpayer types.

**Tax Year:** 2026

## What's Inside

| Calendar | Purpose | Primary Users |
|----------|---------|---------------|
| General Tax Calendar | Key deadlines for all taxpayers | Individuals, businesses, partnerships, corporations |
| Employer's Tax Calendar | Employment tax deadlines | Employers who withhold and pay payroll taxes |
| Excise Tax Calendar | Excise tax filing and deposit dates | Businesses paying excise taxes |

## General Tax Calendar

Covers important due dates for all taxpayers including:

### Individual Deadlines
- **January 15**: Final estimated tax payment for prior year
- **April 15**: Individual income tax return (Form 1040) due; First estimated tax payment
- **June 15**: Second estimated tax payment (also expatriate return deadline)
- **September 15**: Third estimated tax payment
- **October 15**: Extended individual return deadline
- **December 15**: Fourth estimated tax payment for corporations

### Business Deadlines
- **March 16**: Partnership (Form 1065) and S corporation (Form 1120-S) returns due
- **April 15**: C corporation (Form 1120) return due
- **September 15**: Extended partnership and S corporation returns due

### Information Returns
- **February 2**: Furnish Form 1099-NEC to recipients; File Form 1099-NEC with IRS
- **February 17**: Furnish Forms 1099-B and 1099-S to recipients
- **March 2**: File paper Forms 1099 (except 1099-NEC)
- **March 31**: File electronic Forms 1099, 1098, W-2G

## Employer's Tax Calendar

### Primary Employment Tax Forms

| Form | Purpose | Due Date |
|------|---------|----------|
| Form 940 | Federal Unemployment Tax (FUTA) | January 31 |
| Form 941 | Quarterly Federal Tax Return | Last day of month following quarter |
| Form 943 | Agricultural Employment Tax | January 31 |
| Form 944 | Annual Federal Tax Return (small employers) | January 31 |
| Form 945 | Nonpayroll Withholding | January 31 |

### Quarterly Filing Schedule (Form 941)

| Quarter | Months Covered | Due Date |
|---------|----------------|----------|
| Q1 | Jan-Mar | April 30 |
| Q2 | Apr-Jun | July 31 |
| Q3 | Jul-Sep | October 31 |
| Q4 | Oct-Dec | January 31 |

### Extended Due Dates

If you deposited all taxes timely, properly, and in full:
- Form 940: February 10
- Form 941: 10th day of 2nd month after quarter
- Form 943: February 10
- Form 944: February 10
- Form 945: February 10

### Monthly Deposit Schedule

- **Criteria**: Reported $50,000 or less in employment taxes during lookback period
- **Due Date**: 15th day of following month
- Deposit Social Security, Medicare, and withheld income taxes

### Semiweekly Deposit Schedule

- **Criteria**: Reported more than $50,000 in employment taxes during lookback period
- **Due Dates**: Based on payday (see Semiweekly Deposit Table)

### W-2 and Information Reporting

- **February 2**: Furnish Form W-2 to employees; File Form W-3 with Copy A of Forms W-2
- **February 2**: Furnish Form 1099-NEC to recipients; File with IRS
- **March 2**: File Form 8027 (paper); File Forms W-2G (paper)
- **March 31**: File electronic Forms 8027, W-2G

## Excise Tax Calendar

### Primary Excise Tax Forms

| Form | Purpose | Frequency |
|------|---------|-----------|
| Form 720 | Quarterly Federal Excise Tax Return | Quarterly |
| Form 2290 | Heavy Highway Vehicle Use Tax | Annually (August 31) |
| Form 730 | Monthly Tax Return for Wagers | Monthly |
| Form 11-C | Occupational Tax and Registration for Wagering | Annually (July 1) |

### Quarterly Form 720 Due Dates

| Quarter | Months Covered | Due Date |
|---------|----------------|----------|
| Q1 | Jan-Mar | April 30 |
| Q2 | Apr-Jun | July 31 |
| Q3 | Jul-Sep | November 2 |
| Q4 | Oct-Dec | February 2 |

### Regular Method vs. Alternative Method Deposits

- **Regular Method**: Semiweekly deposits for most excise taxes
- **Alternative Method**: Used for communications and air transportation taxes
- **Special September Rule**: Different deposit deadlines apply

### Heavy Highway Vehicle Use Tax (Form 2290)

- **Annual Due Date**: August 31 (for vehicles in use during July)
- **Partial Year**: File by last day of month following first use
- **Tax Period**: July 1 - June 30

## Semiweekly Deposit Table

For employers subject to semiweekly deposit rules:

| Payday Falls On | Deposit Due By |
|-----------------|----------------|
| Wednesday, Thursday, Friday | Following Wednesday |
| Saturday, Sunday, Monday, Tuesday | Following Friday |

### $100,000 Next-Day Rule

If you accumulate $100,000 or more in taxes on any day:
- Deposit by the next business day
- You become a semiweekly depositor for remainder of year and following year

## Fiscal Year Taxpayers

### Individuals (Form 1040)
- Due 15th day of 4th month after tax year ends
- Estimated payments: 15th day of 4th, 6th, 9th months and 1st month after year ends

### Partnerships (Form 1065)
- Due 15th day of 3rd month after tax year ends
- Provide Schedule K-1 to partners by same date

### Corporations (Form 1120)
- Due 15th day of 4th month after tax year ends
- Estimated payments: 15th day of 4th, 6th, 9th, and 12th months

### S Corporations (Form 1120-S)
- Due 15th day of 3rd month after tax year ends
- Provide Schedule K-1 to shareholders by same date

## Saturday, Sunday, Legal Holiday Rules

If a due date falls on a Saturday, Sunday, or legal holiday:
- Deadline is extended to the next business day
- Legal holidays are those observed in the District of Columbia
- Statewide legal holidays affect filing deadlines for residents of that state

## Electronic Filing and Deposits

### Required Electronic Deposits (EFTPS)
- All federal tax deposits must be made electronically
- Use EFTPS, IRS Direct Pay, or business tax account
- Same-day wire through financial institution also available

### Electronic Filing Extensions
- Forms 1099, 1098, W-2G: March 31 (instead of February 28)
- Forms 8027: March 31 (instead of February 28)
- Forms 1094-C/1095-C: March 31 (instead of March 2)
