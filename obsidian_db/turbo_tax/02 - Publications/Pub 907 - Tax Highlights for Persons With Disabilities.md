# Pub 907 - Tax Highlights for Persons With Disabilities

## Summary

IRS Publication 907 provides tax information for individuals with disabilities and those who care for them. It covers income exclusions, tax credits, deductions, ABLE accounts, and business tax incentives related to disability.

## Important Rules

### ABLE Account Contribution Limits (2025)
- Annual limit: $19,000 (gift tax exclusion amount)
- Additional contribution for employed beneficiaries: Lesser of compensation or poverty line amount ($15,650 continental US, $17,990 Hawaii, $19,550 Alaska)

### Disability Pension Tax Treatment
- Report as wages until minimum retirement age (Form 1040, line 1h)
- After minimum retirement age: Report as pension/annuity (lines 5a/5b)
- VA disability benefits are **not taxable**

### Saver's Credit Income Limits (2025)
- Married filing jointly: $79,000
- Head of household: $59,250
- Single/MFS/QSS: $39,500
