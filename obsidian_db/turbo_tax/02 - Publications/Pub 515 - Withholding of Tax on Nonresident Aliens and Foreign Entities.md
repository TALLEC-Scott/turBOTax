# Publication 515 - Withholding of Tax on Nonresident Aliens and Foreign Entities

## Overview

Publication 515 is the comprehensive guide for **withholding agents** who pay income to foreign persons, including nonresident aliens, foreign corporations, foreign partnerships, foreign trusts, foreign estates, foreign governments, and international organizations. It covers the rules for Chapter 3 withholding, Chapter 4 (FATCA) withholding, and reporting obligations on Forms 1042 and 1042-S.

## Key Topics Covered

### Withholding Framework
- **[[Withholding Agent]]** - Definition, responsibilities, and liability for tax
- **[[Chapter 3 Withholding]]** - Standard 30% withholding on U.S. source income to foreign persons
- **[[FATCA Chapter 4 Withholding]]** - Additional withholding requirements for foreign financial institutions

### Income Types and Sourcing
- **[[FDAP Income]]** - Fixed or Determinable Annual or Periodical income subject to withholding
- **[[Effectively Connected Income (ECI)]]** - Income connected with U.S. trade or business
- **[[Source of Income Rules]]** - Determining U.S. vs. foreign source income

### Intermediaries and Entities
- **[[Foreign Intermediaries]]** - Qualified Intermediaries (QI), Nonqualified Intermediaries (NQI), Withholding Foreign Partnerships (WP), and Withholding Foreign Trusts (WT)
- **[[Flow-Through Entities]]** - Foreign partnerships, foreign simple trusts, and foreign grantor trusts

### Documentation and Reporting
- **[[Documentation Requirements (W-8/W-9)]]** - Forms W-8BEN, W-8BEN-E, W-8ECI, W-8EXP, W-8IMY, and W-9
- **[[Forms 1042 and 1042-S Reporting]]** - Annual withholding returns and information reporting

### Special Topics
- **[[Tax Treaties for Withholding]]** - Reduced rates and exemptions under tax treaties
- **[[U.S. Real Property Interest (USRPI)]]** - Withholding on dispositions of U.S. real property
- **[[Partnership Withholding on ECTI]]** - Withholding on effectively connected taxable income

## Statutory Withholding Rates

| Type | Rate | Notes |
|------|------|-------|
| Chapter 3 (default) | 30% | On gross U.S. source FDAP income |
| Chapter 4 (FATCA) | 30% | On withholdable payments to non-participating FFIs |
| Treaty rates | 0-15% | Varies by treaty and income type |
| Foreign private foundation | 4% | On gross investment income |

## What's New for 2025

- **Partial suspension of tax treaties with Russia and Belarus** - Withholding at 30% statutory rate required for payments to recipients who previously qualified for treaty benefits
- **E-filing threshold lowered to 10 returns** - Applies to Forms 1042 and 1042-S for returns required after January 1, 2024
- **Section 1446(f) withholding** - Fully effective for transfers of partnership interests
