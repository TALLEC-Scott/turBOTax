# Publication 544 - Sales and Other Dispositions of Assets

## Summary

IRS Publication 544 explains the tax rules that apply when you dispose of property. It covers how to determine whether your gain or loss is ordinary or capital, how to figure gains and losses on sales and exchanges, and how to report them on your tax return.

## What's New for 2025

- **Qualified production property**: Section 1245 property now includes qualified production property placed in service after July 4, 2025 (P.L. 119-21, "One Big Beautiful Bill Act")
- **Clean vehicle tax credits**: Credits under sections 25E, 30D, and 45W expired September 30, 2025
- **Sound recording productions**: Qualified for section 181 expensing and section 168(k) additional depreciation
- **Form 1099-DA**: New information return for digital asset transactions
