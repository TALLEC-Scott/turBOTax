# Publication 527 - Residential Rental Property (Including Rental of Vacation Homes)

## Summary

IRS Publication 527 provides comprehensive guidance on the tax treatment of residential rental property, including vacation homes. It covers rental income and expenses, depreciation rules, special rental situations, and the rules for properties with both rental and personal use.

## What's New for 2025

- **Standard mileage rate**: 70 cents per mile for operating vehicles for rental activities
- **Bonus depreciation**: 100% special depreciation allowance restored for qualified property acquired and placed in service after January 19, 2025
- **Section 179 deduction limit**: Maximum deduction is $2,500,000; limit reduced by cost exceeding $4,000,000
- **SALT deduction limit**: Increased to $40,000 ($20,000 if married filing separately)
