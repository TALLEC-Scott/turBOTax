# Credit Percentage Agi Thresholds 2025

Applicable percentage rates for the child and dependent care credit based on the taxpayer's Adjusted Gross Income (AGI). The percentage decreases by 1% for each $2,000 (or fraction thereof) of AGI over $15,000.
