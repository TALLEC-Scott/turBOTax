# Blocked Income Tax Formula

Formula table for calculating estimated U.S. tax on blocked (nonconvertible foreign currency) income for Fulbright grantees. Shows how to determine the portion of tax that can be paid in foreign currency when at least 70% of a Fulbright grant is paid in nonconvertible foreign currency.
