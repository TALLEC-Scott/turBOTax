# Includible Compensation Worksheet

Worksheet B for calculating includible compensation for the most recent year of service, which is used to determine the limit on annual additions for 403(b) plans. Includes wages, elective deferrals, cafeteria plan contributions, 457 plan deferrals, transportation fringe benefits, and foreign earned income exclusion.
